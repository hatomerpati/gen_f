Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing Gen F Qur'an. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure and personalized experience. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information through the Gen F Qur'an app.

1. Information We Collect


  • Location Data: Gen F Qur'an may request access to your location data to provide location-based features, such as displaying prayer times based on your current location. This data is collected only while you’re actively using the app and is not stored on our servers.
  • Google Account Information: For login and subscription purposes, we require access to your Google account. This includes your Google ID, profile name, and email address, which are used to authenticate your identity and manage subscriptions.

2. How We Use Your Information


  • To Provide Core Features: Location data is used to enhance the app’s functionality, such as providing accurate prayer times and other location-relevant information.
  • Account Management: Your Google account information allows you to access the app, manage your subscriptions, and restore your access across devices.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure


  • We do not share your personaW data with third parties, except as required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others.
  • Aggregated, non-identifiable data may be used to improve app performance and user experience.

4. Data Security


  • We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Sensitive information, such as your Google account details, is stored securely and only accessible by authorized personnel.

5. User Control and Choices


  • Location Permissions: You can manage location permissions through your device settings. However, disabling location access may affect certain app features.
  • Google Account Login: You can manage or revoke Google account permissions through your Google account settings.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy


  • We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted in the app and take effect immediately.

7. Contact Us


  • If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at genfway@gmail.com .
